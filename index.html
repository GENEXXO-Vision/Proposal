Company Overview

What Is GENEXXO?

Genexxo Vision Limited is an Isle of Man incorporated company whose mission is to deliver the organising layer for the internet. The foundation is the GENEXXO Collection — over 10,000 XX-branded .com domains assembled over 14 years, spanning 45 major sectors and 400+ sub-categories. The Collection is the asset that makes the Vision possible.

Every domain follows the same signature: a single generic English word paired with the XX suffix — creating branded, category-defining .com addresses. Names like HealthXX.com, FinanceXX.com, TechXX.com, FashionXX.com, TravelXX.com, LawXX.com and thousands more across every significant industry on earth. Assembled, verified, and held within GVL.

The internet needs an organising layer. The GENEXXO Collection — built domain by domain, sector by sector, over fourteen years — is the asset that makes it possible. Nothing comparable exists.
